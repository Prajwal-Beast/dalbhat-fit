02 · Recommendation

Lead with Concept 01

Recommended mark

Pulse Peak — the heartbeat summit

It says fitness at a glance — a heartbeat every gym-goer recognises — while the tall central spike doubles as a Himalayan peak, keeping the design unmistakably Nepali. It collapses to a single continuous stroke, so it stays sharp on a watch face, a tab bar, or an embroidered cap.

  • Fitness-first — the pulse reads instantly as health, energy, and training.
  • Subtly Nepali — the summit spike + saffron marker nod to the mountains, no literal illustration.
  • Ultra-scalable — one stroke survives down to 16px and one-colour print. Concept 02 (In Motion) is the energetic backup for marketing.
