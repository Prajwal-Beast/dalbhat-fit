Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

DhalBhat Fit ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information when you use the DhalBhat Fit mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you create an account (via email/password or Google Sign-In).
  • Health and fitness data: Height, weight, age, gender, fitness goals, activity level, and daily food logs you enter in the App.
  • Usage data: Which features you use within the App (stored locally on your device).
  • Device information: Basic device identifiers used for app functionality (not sold to third parties).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and personalise the App's features (calorie targets, workout recommendations, progress tracking).
  • Sync your data securely across sessions so you never lose your progress.
  • Calculate your BMI, TDEE, and daily calorie targets using standard fitness formulas.
  • Improve the App based on aggregated, anonymised usage patterns.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS in the Asia Pacific region). All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Row-level security ensures only you can access your own data.

A copy of your food logs is also stored locally on your device using SQLite for offline functionality. This local data stays on your device and is not shared.

4. Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google. We do not receive your Google password or any other Google account data. Your use of Google Sign-In is also governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Upon deletion, all your personal data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

DhalBhat Fit is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct any inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Export your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

8. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Sign-In — optional authentication (Privacy Policy)

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
