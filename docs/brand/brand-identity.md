## Design System Rules

- Border radius: 16–20px on cards, 12px on buttons, 8px on chips
- Card shadows: `BoxShadow(color: #0000001A, blurRadius: 8, offset: Offset(0, 2))`
- No hard drop shadows — use soft, low-opacity shadows only
- No gradients except the calorie ring (subtle green gradient)
- Icons: Lucide Icons or Material Symbols (consistent set, not mixed)
- No stock photography — flat illustration or none
- Minimum tap target: 48×48px (accessibility)
- Minimum text contrast: 4.5:1 (WCAG AA)

## Brand Voice

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| "You're 300 kcal under today. Have a snack?" | "Don't forget to eat enough!" |
| "7 days logged. Consistency is everything." | "Amazing job superstar! 🎉🎉" |
